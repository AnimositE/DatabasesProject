DiveBook is a user-oriented experience that gives functionality and usability to collegiate divers.

Who We Are

A project team for Rose-Hulman's databases course creating a solution to a real problem. The team consists of Si Fi Faye Li (Project Creator, Developer, and Designer), Mason Schneider (Developer, Server Manager, and Database Administrator), and Mark Hein (Developer and Query Optimizer)

What We Do

DiveBook is a diver database to streamline the dive meet process. Currently in a Dive meet, everything is handled through hand written forms and scores are calculated manually through the use of large tables. DiveBook will expedite this process and take stress off both the divers and the judges. It will also allow easier record keeping which is a prevalent annoyance involved in current dive meets.

Features

One major purpose of DiveBook is Degree of Difficulty lookup for dives. DiveBook also allows divers to complete, store, and share dive sheets. This makes the process of dive meets much easier, as DiveBook almost completely eliminates typos and errors that can cause disqualifications. DiveBook will track divers history, score statistics, and personal information.
